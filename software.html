🔧 Free Essential Tools

Must-have software that every sim racer should install

Free

SimHub

The ultimate dashboard and telemetry tool. SimHub grabs data from your racing game and displays it however you want: external screens, custom overlays, and hardware integration for bass shakers and LED displays. Completely free and endlessly customizable.

Continuously updated
Free

Crew Chief

A voice-activated spotter and race engineer that calls gaps, warns about blue flags, monitors tyre temperatures, estimates fuel, and flags incidents. One of the most beloved apps in the community, and it's completely free.

Continuously updated
Free

Content Manager (AC)

The essential companion for Assetto Corsa. Content Manager replaces the default launcher with a feature-rich interface for managing mods, servers, car setups, and graphics settings. Custom Shaders Patch integration transforms AC's visuals.

Ongoing

🤖 AI Coaching & Performance

AI-powered tools that help you find those last tenths

AI

Delta

A comprehensive platform offering AI coaching, automatic setups, leaderboards, data analysis, and the Never Lift driving course. One subscription covers seven sim titles, making it excellent value for multi-sim racers.

2026
AI

Track Titan

Corner-by-corner AI analysis that compares your driving line, braking points, and throttle application against faster drivers. Provides specific, actionable advice for each corner of every track.

2026
AI

Trophi.ai

The newest entrant in AI coaching, Trophi.ai focuses on real-time feedback during driving sessions. Analyzes your telemetry and provides suggestions as you drive, accelerating the learning curve.

2026

📈 Data Analysis

Professional-grade telemetry and performance analysis

Pro

MoTeC i2

The professional standard used in real motorsport. MoTeC i2 offers deep telemetry analysis with channel math, overlay comparisons, and custom workspaces. The most powerful tool available, with a learning curve to match.

Industry Standard
Partnership

Cosworth Pi Toolbox Partners with iRacing

Cosworth's Pi Toolbox is now an official data analysis partner of iRacing, bringing professional-grade telemetry to over 350,000 active sim racers. Live remote telemetry support allows critical data transmission during sessions for immediate engineering input.

2026
Tool

Second Monitor / RaceLab

Overlay and HUD tools that display real-time race information on a second screen: standings, relative gaps, fuel calculations, tyre data, and pit strategy. Essential for competitive endurance racing.

2026
